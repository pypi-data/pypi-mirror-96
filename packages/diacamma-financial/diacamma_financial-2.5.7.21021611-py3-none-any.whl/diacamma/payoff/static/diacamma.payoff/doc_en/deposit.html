Depositing checks¶

Since the menu Financial/Deposit splits, you can add and view checks slips.

In a slip sheet, you can select payments made by check in your various bills. This is a bundle of checks you that you can deposit to your bank branch. Once completed, are closing the defined selection.

You can then print the check deposit slip that you can attach to your bundle of depositing it. In addition, once your note appears on your account statement, you can validate all your accounting entries from the plug itself.
