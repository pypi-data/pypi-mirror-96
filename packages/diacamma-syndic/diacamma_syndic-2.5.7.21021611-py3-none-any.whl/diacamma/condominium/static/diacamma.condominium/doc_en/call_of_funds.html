Call of funds¶

To create a call for funds, click on the menu General/condominium/call of funds. From this list of long distance calls, you can create a new one via the “Add” button.

After having specified the date and description of the call, a new call record is created. Add in this card different call elements. Each one you specify on all batches associated with this element of appeal and the amount.

Finally, to complete the cross-call, click “Confirm” All owners will then see associated with a new form of cross-appeal. Every amounts previously seized items are changed according to the ratio of the whole that each co-owner owns.
